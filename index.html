Get ready for some Halloween fun at Marlboro High School’s Trunk or Treat event! Presented by the Peer Leaders, this spooktacular celebration will be held on October 29th from 4:00 to 5:30 p.m. in the school parking lot. Kids 12 and under are invited to wear their best costumes and enjoy over 30 decorated trunks full of treats. Don’t worry if it rains—there’s a backup date set for October 30th! This is a nut-free event, ensuring a safe experience for everyone. Plus, snacks and refreshments will be available for purchase, with proceeds going to benefit the American Cancer Society. Bring your Halloween spirit and help support a great cause!
